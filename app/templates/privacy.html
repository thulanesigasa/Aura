Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using the Aura platform, such as when you search for Spaza shops, create an account, or contact support. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and location data to show nearby shops.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the Aura platform. This includes personalizing your experience, providing customer support, and communicating with you about updates or offers related to local Spaza shops.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@aura.co.za.
